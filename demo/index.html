  --use-case bank_aml \
  --patterns mule_account,structuring,layering \
  --format all \

What it does

Creates realistic fraud scenarios

Model mule accounts, account takeover, velocity abuse, fraud rings, KYC abuse, marketplace refunds, chargebacks, structuring, layering, and cross-border anomalies.

Why it matters

Reduces risky test data habits

Teams can test dashboards, review queues, rule engines, APIs, reports, and ML pipelines without copying production records or exposing sensitive identifiers.

Impact

Speeds up fraud product work

Product, data, compliance, QA, and engineering teams get repeatable fixtures that make demos clearer, regressions easier, and fraud-risk workflows faster to validate.
